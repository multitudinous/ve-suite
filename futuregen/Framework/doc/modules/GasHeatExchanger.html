Gas Heat Exchanger Module

The Gas Heat Exchanger module allows the user to heat or cool a gas stream to a desired temperature. The module calculates both the heat transfer necessary to bring the gas stream to the specified temperature, and also outputs the resultant Temperature Change between inlet and outlet streams.

Connections

  • Inlet Port 0: Inlet gas stream.
  • Outlet Port 0: Outlet gas stream of desired temperature.

Graphical User Interface (UI)

The desired outlet gas temperature is input here.


Summary Data (SUM)

Upon completion, the total heat energy transfered, Q (expressed in MW), as well as the resultant Temperature Change expressed in Kelvin.  Negative values indicate that heat was removed from the gas stream.
