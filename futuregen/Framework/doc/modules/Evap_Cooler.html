Evaporator Cooler Module

The evaporation cooler model is used to simulate a cooling tower for cooling the heated liquid water from the condenser via a direct contact heat exchange evaporation process. The ambient relative humidity and desired outlet water temperature are included as inputs. Model calculations range from the required air flow to evaporated water loss from the process.

Connections

  • Inlet Port 0: Inlet water stream.
  • Outlet Port 0 : Cooled outlet water stream.

Graphical User Interface (UI)

User inputs Desired Temperature of water stream (K) and ambient air properties such as Temperature, Relative Humidity, and Pressure.


Summary Data (SUM)

Following a sucessful model run the summary table provides: Air Flow Required (kg/s) in order to obtain desired temperature and the resultant Evaporation Rate  (kg/s) associated with the tower.
